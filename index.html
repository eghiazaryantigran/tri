WELCOME TO TRICOLOR

Tri Color Imaging is a Professional Digital Imaging Lab. Headquartered in Medford, Long Island, our mission is to provide professional photographers with the tools and resources they need to realize their visions and fulfill their clients’ needs. We are a family business, Founded 42 years ago by Nick Melconian. a family business quickly took shape as some of his siblings soon joined Nick at Tri Color Imaging dedicated to servicing the professional photography industry.
Several of their children eventually entered the business creating continuity of ownership while further developing the strong bond of family while Tri Color continued to grow. Tri Color is a family business in the most positive way possible. A sense of loyalty, closeness, warmth and well being extends to all the Tri Color employees…, literally creating one very large “family”…yet that is only a part of the Tri Color story. Tri Color was founded on very basic core beliefsthat were destined to assure our success, mainly: to provide our customers with the highest quality professional products and services at the lowest costs possible to ensure that a truly profitable relationship will exist for all. HIGH QUALITY plus LOW COST simply equals … TRUE VALUE. The Tri Color “family” bond among all our employees is a strong one … but perhaps of equal importance is the “family” bond that extends and exists between our customers and us. This is a natural result considering our basic philosophy, but also, from the earliest start-up days, forty years ago, andevident throughout the ensuing years, has been our steadfast commitment to SERVICE. We know that you, the professional image maker, needs to count on your imaging services provider to be thoroughly RELIABLE, to be RESPONSIVE to your needs,and to be able to instill in youthe CONFIDENCE from knowing that your back is covered while we do everything possible to help you look as good as possible with your clients. Our customers have a strong sense of TRUST in us, so much so, that they feel they all are most welcome members of our “family” … the Tri Color Family. We strive to support you in every way possible and we extend to you our warm invitation to also become a part of the Tri Color Family of Professionals!!!
